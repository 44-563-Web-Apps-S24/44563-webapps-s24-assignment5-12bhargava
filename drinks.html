Elachi Tea

As the name suggests, elaichai chai or cardamom tea literally translates to cardamom flavoured tea. The flavor of the tea is very powerful, it is basically made by brewing black tea leaves with cardamom. We have made cardamom tea by combining water, tea powder, sugar and crushed cardamom and boiled it together
